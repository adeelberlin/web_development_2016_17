How to get here

  • From Goldsmiths university head towards new cross gate.
  • Then take the over ground train to highbury and islington.
  • Get out at canada water and take the jubilee line towards stanmore.
  • Get out at waterloo and head for the exit.
  • Follow signs to Mirror Dome Theme Park.
